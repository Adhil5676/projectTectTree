Frequently Asked Questions

Explore some common questions about solar services and electrical work

✔ What are the benefits of solar panel installation? +
Solar panels reduce electricity bills, provide clean energy, and increase property value.
✔ How long does a solar panel installation take? +
It typically takes 1-3 days to install, depending on the system size and weather conditions.
➖ What maintenance is required for solar panels? -
Regular cleaning and periodic inspections are recommended to ensure optimal performance.
✔ Do solar panels work in cloudy or rainy conditions? +
Yes, but their efficiency may be slightly reduced during cloudy or rainy weather.
✔ What financing options are available for solar installations? +
Various financing options such as loans, leases, and government incentives are available.
